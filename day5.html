Final Challenge - Have Some Fun

Create web page about your favorite musical artist. Your page should include the following:

  • A navbar with at least four items.(Tour Dates, Biography, Discograhy, Image Gallery, etc.) The navbar items should be evenly spaced and centered. The navbar should stay pinned to the top of the screen even when scrolling the page. (Positioning)
  • One CSS animation that plays on its own and repeats forever.(CSS Animation)
  • One CSS animation that responds to clicking or hovering over. (CSS Events)
  • NOTE: your animations should move smoothly.
  • A box with a border, an image inside, a caption on the image, and padding around the image and text. (Box Model)
  • BONUS: Add two or more of the above boxes and make them live next to each other on the page. (Inline-Block)
  • Add at least one "quiz question" with an answer that will appear when a button is clicked. (Javascript)
  • Add at least one button that will cause a popup message to appear on the screen. (Javascript)

Final Challenge - Have Some Fun

Create a page with the follow items:

  • Two boxes, one in each bottom corner of the screen. The boxes should stay on the corners of the screen even if you scroll up or down.
  • HINT: to make your screen scroll just keeping addinv boxes (DIVS!)
  • Add two boxes, one in each top corner of the screen. When hovered over, the boxes should move to the opposite corner (left to right and vice-versa). When clicked on, the boxes should change to a different color.
  • BONUS: Make the boxes smoothly change back to their original colors and position when no longer clicked or hovered.
  • A box with a border, an image inside, a caption on the image, and padding around the image and text.
  • BONUS: Add two or more of the above boxes and make them live next to each other on the page.
  • Add at least one button that will pop-up a JavaScript alert when clicked on.
  • BONUS: Add at least one button that changes an HTML element when clicked on.
